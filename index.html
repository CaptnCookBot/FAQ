1️⃣How to start?
▼

When you first launch the bot, simply type /start.

The bot will automatically generate your personal wallet and display your wallet address along with your SOL balance.

From the main menu, you can access all features: Buy, Sell, Positions, Settings, Withdraw, and Referral.

💰
2️⃣How to deposit SOL?
▼

After using /start, your wallet address is displayed.

To fund your account, simply send SOL from your exchange or personal wallet directly to this address.

⚠️ Warning: Always double-check the address before sending.
📈
3️⃣How to buy tokens?
▼

Buying tokens is very simple:

  1. Paste the token's Contract Address (CA) directly into the chat.
  2. The bot will automatically load the token details (price, market cap, liquidity, etc).
  3. You will see several preset buttons allowing you to buy with predefined amounts (e.g. 0.1 SOL, 0.5 SOL, 1 SOL, etc).
  4. You can also manually enter a custom amount by clicking ✏️ X SOL.
📉
4️⃣How to sell tokens?
▼

After buying a token:

  1. Select the token from your positions.
  2. You will see several preset sell buttons based on percentages (10%, 25%, 50%, 75%, 100%).
  3. You can also click on ✏️ X % to manually enter any custom percentage to sell.
📊
5️⃣How to check my PNL?
▼

To generate a PNL image for a trade:

Use the command:
/pnl [CONTRACT_ADDRESS]

Example: /pnl 6MQpbiTC2YcogidTmKqMLK82qvE9z5QEm7EP3AEDpump
ℹ️ Note: The bot will generate a PNL image only if you have traded this token.
⚡
6️⃣How to configure fees?
▼

Go to Settings → Fees

You can choose between 3 preset modes:

  • Normal
  • Fast
  • Turbo (highest priority)

Higher fees allow faster transactions by prioritizing your transaction on-chain.

You can also set a custom fee by clicking Custom Fees and entering your own amount (in SOL) for both buy and sell fees.

🎯
7️⃣How to configure slippage?
▼

Go to Settings → Slippage

Slippage determines the price difference you're willing to accept between placing the order and execution.

You can set different slippages for Buy and Sell operations.

📊 Recommended values:
• For low market cap / highly volatile tokens: 15% - 40%
• For safer tokens: lower slippage is enough.
🤖
8️⃣How to activate AutoBuy?
▼

AutoBuy allows you to automatically buy a token when a CA is posted:

Go to Settings → AutoBuy

You can:

  • Enable or disable AutoBuy.
  • Set the amount of SOL you want the bot to automatically buy with.
⚙️
9️⃣How to configure Buy/Sell Presets?
▼

Presets are the quick-buy/sell buttons you see under each token.

Go to Settings → Presets

You can customize the preset amounts for both Buy and Sell to fit your strategy.

You have 5 Buy presets and 5 Sell presets available.

👛
🔟How to manage my Wallet?
▼

Go to Settings → Wallet

You will see:

  • Your wallet address
  • Your balance
  • Your Seed Phrase

You can:

  • Import an existing wallet
  • Create a new wallet
⚠️ NEVER share your seed phrase with anyone!

⚠️ Be careful: importing or creating a new wallet will delete the previous one stored on CaptainCook. Always back up your seed phrase before proceeding.
📋
