📘 Captain Cook Bot – FAQ

Welcome to the official FAQ for Captain Cook, your Solana-based trading assistant.
From setting up your wallet to executing memecoin trades, everything you need is below.

⚙️ How do I set up the bot?

Use the /start command in Telegram. A wallet is generated and linked to your Telegram ID.

💰 How do I buy a token?

Use /buy $TOKEN or tap the "Buy" button after a call. The bot will show price and fees before confirming.

📈 What is market cap filtering?

Free users only see calls under 100k MC. VIP users get calls up to 5M MC with scoring and PnL tracking.

🔐 How secure is my wallet?

Each wallet is generated locally and stored encrypted. You can export your seed phrase at any time using /backup.
